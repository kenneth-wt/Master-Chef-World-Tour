**MasterChef: World Tour** is a first-person VR cooking simulator inspired by the MasterChef TV show. Players step into a high-pressure kitchen to prepare iconic dishes and are judged on taste, presentation, and hygiene.  

The project aimed to deliver a **realistic, immersive cooking experience** using VR mechanics, responsive tools, and interactive judge feedback. The final version features the USA challenge (Cheeseburger) as a complete, polished experience showcasing realistic food preparation, plating, and evaluation.
